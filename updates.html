How do I subscribe to emails?

You just have to send our address an email with the title of "subscribe". It's interpreted by a machine, so please don't add more details like "please" or "thanks" - it won't work if you do! In fact, you can do that right here and now!




What are the emails like?

The emails contain the standard explanation - Prague Race has updated, you are given a link to the Prague Race website and an unsubscribe mailto link. They contain hidden spoilers, too. The update name and time (visible only when highlighted), and the last panel as an image (visible only when dragged). You can't and won't see them accidentally, but they're handy to keep in touch with the latest plotlines even if you're short on time.




How do I unsubscribe?

It's as simple as subscribing! You have to send an email titled "unsubscribe" to the same address. I, the creator of this, would really appreciate it if you gave additional input on what you think of PRUN and why you unsubscribed.

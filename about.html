What does this do?

The answer is in the name - PRUN - Prague Race Update Notifier. The PRUN system allows users to subscribe to email notifications for updates to the comic Prague Race, and displays the important details of the latest update: Its title, time and a peek at the new panel.




Why not just use the Prague Race RSS?

Feeds are often a few minutes late - they can get to fifteen minutes of delay. They may require copy-pasting URLs or code, and it's ugly and error-prone. They're uncomfortable and constricting, making you feel in a hurry to see everything and never miss a single thing. They offer very limited info; they lack the panel (the most important part!) and instead show "today's news" that Leppu seems to have dropped.

PRUN aims to fix all of that. It is designed in a way to reduce stress on the servers while maintaining quick update recognition: Under 120 seconds. PRUN doesn't require an external program to be downloaded like most readers do, or even a site visit. Instead, it offers emails - you can get notified on desktop, laptop, mobile, or even a smart watch. The PRUN website and emails contain all the information you'd want about the update.
